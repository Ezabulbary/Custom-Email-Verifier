FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does BounceCure send actual emails to verify addresses? +
No, absolutely not. We use SMTP handshake technology — we knock on the mail server's door and ask if the mailbox exists, but we never actually send an email. This keeps your sender reputation completely clean.
What is a Catch-All email and why does it matter? +
A catch-all server accepts every incoming email regardless of whether the specific mailbox exists. Standard verifiers mark all of these as "valid," leading to bounces. BounceCure uses a special random-probe test to identify and flag these separately.
How long do my credits last? +
Your purchased credits never expire. Use them at your own pace — whether that's today or six months from now. There are no monthly minimums or subscription fees.
Can I integrate BounceCure into my own application? +
Yes! We offer a simple REST API with Bearer Token authentication. Simply register, get your token from the dashboard, and start making API calls. Documentation is available in your dashboard under "API Docs."
What payment methods do you accept? +
We use Stripe for secure payments. You can pay with any major credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), as well as Google Pay and Apple Pay where available.
What formats can I upload for bulk verification? +
You can upload a standard CSV file with an email column, paste emails directly (one per line) into our bulk verifier, or send a JSON array via our API. Our engine automatically detects the email column in your CSV.
